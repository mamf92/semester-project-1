Getting here

Getting here by:

Public transport

The easiest way to get here by public transport i by bus. You have plenty of lines to choose from all stopping at either University East Entrance or Arts Center, a 3 min walk from the museum.

Use lines: 18, 18a, 18b, 34, 34C, 53, 53B, 54, 54B or indigo

Plan with Google Maps

Getting here by:

Car

Arriving by car? Use our car park located just outside the museum. In holidays and weekends the car park might be busy and we open our extra location only a 3 min walk from the entrance. We can also recommend taking the bus, and easy option.
