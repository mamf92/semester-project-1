There is a shark in this picture

Look at the image, then let your eyes relax, zone out, focus on something far beyond the screen you are looking at. Let your eyes drift slightly. Do you see it? A shark with a fin, popping out at you? No? Try again.

This is a classical optical illusion where you let a 2D image become 3D by making your eyes see one version of the image each. Cool right?
